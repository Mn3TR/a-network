Anna has a small dog. His name is Max. Every morning, Anna takes Max for a walk. They go to the park near their house. In the park, Max likes to run and play with a red ball. Anna throws the ball, and Max brings it back. One day, the ball rolled into a pond. Max jumped in and got it. He came out wet and shook the water off. Anna laughed and gave him a hug. They went home happy.
